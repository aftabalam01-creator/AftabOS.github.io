About Our Founder
Aftab Alam Masjidi
Founder & Technical Lead
Stamps Scholar · Barry University Miami · Summa Cum Laude

Aftab Alam Masjidi founded AftabOS after completing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science, Honors, Summa Cum Laude at Barry University in Miami, Florida, where he was awarded the Stamps Scholarship — the university's highest merit distinction. Trained in AI, cybersecurity, and full-stack systems engineering, Aftab returned his expertise to the Afghan market, building enterprise-grade software for a region that has long been underserved by modern technology. AftabOS operates from both Miami and Kabul, combining American engineering discipline with deep Afghan market knowledge.
